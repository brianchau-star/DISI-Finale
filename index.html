Diagnosing Immediately Symptomatic Illness

The DISI project aims to support healthcare organizations and authorities in proactively predicting the spread of potential infectious diseases early on to promptly implement control measures, prevention, and avoid widespread outbreaks like the Covid-19 pandemic. Additionally, the project also offers support for the general public in terms of online medical care, health monitoring, and early warnings about health conditions based on analyses from algorithms combined with artificial intelligence.
